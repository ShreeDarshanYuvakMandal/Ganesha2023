About Us


Shree Darshan yuvak Mandal established in 2014, is one of the social, well known and active group of Vadodara, located at T.P 13 Road Chhani Jakat Naka, Vadodara, Gujarat. It consists of more than 20 energetic members. Nishit Patel leads the community with his excellent insights and hard-work being a community leader. Each member of the Shree Darshan yuvak Mandal has an important role to play, which they contribute with all their heart and faith in Lord Ganesha. We have received immense appreciation and support from outside masses which has motivated us further to better our work in God. We also have encouraged many to join us and contribute, through these years.
